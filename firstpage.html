Chapter 1

Link and Zelda were in a cave beneath Hyrule castle. Zelda found a lot of murals and she took pictures of them with the Purah Pad. And Link's Master Sword started glowing, and they went farther and they saw a mysterious black mummy. The mummy broke the Master Sword. It became decayed, and one pointy piece (the top of the master sword) cut the mummy's face. And then the mummy took Link's hearts down to three, and Zelda fell.
